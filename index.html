Overview of Crystal Bhubaneswar

Crystal is an exclusive boutique apartment project located in Raghunathpur, Bhubaneswar, offering a perfect blend of luxury, comfort, and convenience. Developed by Adiilaksmi Construction, a trusted name in real estate with over 15 years of experience, Crystal is designed to provide a premium living experience with meticulously crafted 2BHK and 3BHK apartments.

Approved by the Bhubaneswar Development Authority (BDA) and registered under the Odisha Real Estate Regulatory Authority (ORERA), Crystal ensures transparency, legal compliance, and superior construction quality. The project boasts a strategic location, offering seamless connectivity to educational institutions, hospitals, business hubs, and recreational centers in Bhubaneswar.

With modern architecture, high-end specifications, and world-class amenities, Crystal redefines boutique luxury living. The thoughtfully designed homes feature spacious balconies, premium flooring, stylish kitchens, and top-quality fittings, creating an environment that blends sophistication with functionality.

Key Highlights of Crystal

  • Prime Location: Close to schools, hospitals, and commercial zones in Raghunathpur.
  • Premium 2BHK & 3BHK Apartments: Designed for modern, elegant living.
  • ORERA-Registered & BDA Approved: Ensuring transparency and quality.
  • Luxury Amenities: Ample parking, power backup, 24/7 security, CCTV, and more.
  • Sustainable & Smart Living: Energy-efficient design, high-end materials, and smart security features.

At Crystal, we don’t just build homes—we create a lifestyle of elegance and exclusivity. Come, experience a new standard of living in Bhubaneswar’s most sought-after neighborhood.
